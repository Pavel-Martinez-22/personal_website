About Me

My name is Pavel Martinez, and I am currently a teacher for USD 259. I mainly teach introductory computer courses like Photoshop, Premiere, HTML & CSS. I have been teaching for seven years and I really enjoy what I do! I am a part-time student at Butler Community College and by taking this course I hope to advance my skills to better teach my content.

Some of my hobbies include playing video games, tinkering with Adobe software, and watching YouTube videos on new technology. I am very technology focused and I always like to learn about the newest technology out there. Sometimes it can get overwhelming when it is not working but it is always fun to investigate the settings and see what options developers have added. It fascinates me how technology is always evolving and making life a little simpler.
